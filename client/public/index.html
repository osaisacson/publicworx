About

Take active part in the green revolution

We are a collaboration of gardeners and makers turning cities green one balcony/rooftop at a time.

We cheaply set up and help maintain your urban garden - making growing your own food easy and affordable.

By hiring us you don't just get a green rooftop (with associated tax benefits), reduce your food costs (by average 10%), and decrease food transports - you become part of our urban farmer network and actively join the green revolution.

Our gardens go from the very small to the very large. It all depends on the space, conditions and budget available.

Regardless of size our gardens always produce food, save money and reduce transport.

Sometimes they also produce electricity, reduce sewage, create soil, collect water and grow fish.

Small

(up to 2m2)

Your very small balcony or indoor space

You can always grow something.
Most common here is either to grow herbs, microgreens or act as part of our nursery.

Medium

(2 - 100m2)

Your rooftop or normal balcony

Common setups here include grow boxes and self watering systems. This is for when you want to produce part of your own food.

Large

(100m2 and above)

A whole building of integrated systems

Usually involves a mix between growing boxes, rain collection, self watering, compost, and aquaponic (fish growing) systems.
